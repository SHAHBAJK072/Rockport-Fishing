Our Beginning

Welcome to Rockport Fishing Company where a passion for the great outdoors and the constant search for the best fishing gear and clothes. Established in 2024 by Jon Paul who spends most weekend in Rockport Texas and occasionally gets to spend time with friends who have a home in Rockport Maine. Jon Paul proudly hails from the historic town of Victoria, TX which is less than an hour from Rockport Tx.

At Rockport Fishing Company, we're not just another outdoor apparel company; we're a lifestyle brand dedicated to crafting exceptional products that elevate your adventures. From our premium selection of custom hats to a wide array of performance-driven outdoor gear, every product is meticulously designed to enhance your outdoor experiences and celebrate your love for great clothes, excellent gear, and amazing custom hats and shirts.
